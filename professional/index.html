About Us

At Kelley Adventures, we live for the crisp mountain air, the rustle of wind through alpine pines, and the adrenaline rush of exploring uncharted trails. Whether you’re seeking peaceful hikes or heart-pounding climbs, our guided Utah mountain experiences are crafted for thrill-seekers and serenity-chasers alike. Let us be your compass to unforgettable moments, breathtaking views, and stories you'll retell for a lifetime. Adventure starts here—are you ready to answer the call?




Paddle Boarding

Discover serene waters and stunning views with our specialty paddle boarding adventures. Starting at just $50, enjoy two paddle board rentals and a guided trip to one of Utah’s best-kept secret spots. Need more time or gear? Add an extra paddle board for only $15 or extend your rental time for just $10 per hour. Adventure, relaxation, and breathtaking landscapes await—let’s paddle out!

Rock Climbing

Take your love for the outdoors to new heights! Our rock climbing package offers a guided experience on some of Utah’s most breathtaking cliffs and crags. Starting at $75, you'll get expert instruction, climbing gear, and access to routes suited for all skill levels—whether you’re a first-timer or a seasoned climber. Push your limits, soak in the stunning mountain views, and leave with memories (and stories) that will last a lifetime. Adventure is waiting—are you ready to climb?

Hiking

Experience the beauty of Utah’s rugged trails like never before. Starting at $40, our hiking package includes a guided trek through some of the most stunning and secluded paths in the state. From sweeping vistas to hidden waterfalls, every step brings you closer to nature’s wonders. Whether you’re a seasoned explorer or just looking for a peaceful escape, we tailor each hike to suit your pace and interests. Lace up your boots and let’s hit the trail—adventure is calling!
